What We Do

Streamlined ERP Solution

Taka Register offers a comprehensive desktop ERP system tailored for weaving mill operations. It enables business owners and managers to efficiently monitor production workflows, manage yarn inventories, and generate detailed reports.

🧵

Production Tracking

Monitor TDS cards, warping, and loom-wise production in real time.

📦

Yarn Inventory

Track yarn purchases, dyeing, twisting, and stock across all locations.

🎨

Yarn Dyeing

Manage dyeing orders, track receipts, and monitor pending and completed jobs.

🧶

Fabric Dyeing

Track fabric sent for dyeing, processing status, and stock at processor units.

📊

Reports & Analysis

Yarn consumption reports, weaver stock summaries, and closure wizards.

🔒

Offline & Secure

Fully desktop-based with local SQLite storage — no cloud dependency.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Taka Register is a desktop application that stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit personal data to our servers. The following data may be accessed:

  • Google Account Data: If you enable Google Drive backup, we request access to your Google Drive solely to store and retrieve your backup files. We do not read, modify, or share any other Google account data.
  • Local Business Data: All mill operational data (weavers, purchases, production records) is stored in a local SQLite database on your machine.
  • Mobile App: The Taka Register mobile app uses Google Sign-In to access the same Google Drive backup created by the desktop app, so you can view your mill data and submit new orders on the go. No data is sent to Taka Register's own servers from the mobile app either.

If you connect your Google account for Drive backup, Taka Register will:

  • Create and access only the backup files created by this application in your Google Drive.
  • Never read, modify, or share any other files in your Google Drive.
  • Never use your Google data for advertising or any purpose beyond the backup feature.
  • Store your OAuth tokens locally on your device only.

Taka Register's use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

All application data is stored locally on your Windows device. No data is transmitted to or stored on Taka Register servers. Backup files sent to Google Drive are under the control of your Google account and subject to Google's own privacy policy.

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. The only external service used is Google Drive (optional, user-initiated backup only).

Since all data resides locally on your device, you can delete the application and its database at any time. To revoke Google Drive access, remove the app from your Google Account permissions.

Taka Register is a business application not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@takaregister.in.
